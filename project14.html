        so it is important to master this crucial talent. Here are some vital tricks you should know:
Coping
In case you come across important info coping will be easier.

To copy press: Ctrl + C

Pasting
After copying your data you can paste it where ever you want.

To paste press: ctrl + v

Printing
If you ever come across something you want to print on paper use this.

To print press: ctrl + p
